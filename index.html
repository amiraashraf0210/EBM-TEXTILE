Prime Minister Visit

A Historic Milestone for Port Said's Industrial Development

On May 10, 2025, we were honored to welcome His Excellency Dr. Mostafa Madbouly, the Prime Minister of Egypt, during his official visit to Port Said Governorate. This historic visit marked a significant milestone in recognizing the industrial development and economic growth of the region.

During his visit, the Prime Minister inspected and inaugurated several development and service projects in the East and West Port Said areas, which are part of the Suez Canal Economic Zone. The visit highlighted the strategic importance of Port Said as a key industrial hub and export gateway for Egypt.

The Prime Minister was accompanied by distinguished officials including Engineer Kamel El-Wazir, Deputy Prime Minister for Industrial Development and Minister of Industry and Transport, and Admiral Osama Rabie, Chairman of the Suez Canal Authority.
